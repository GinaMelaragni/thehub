The Business HUB at Endicott College

What is the HUB?

  1. A lab space space for students to up-skill and self-teach skills required for “dream” Internships
  2. A dedicated place to learn in demand Business software apps
  3. Conversations and meet-ups with alums to learn about previous internship sites
  4. Work with mentors, peers and Internship Faculty to sharpen your interviewing skills
  5. Utilize resources online and in the HUB to perfect your resume
  6. Advice on anything internship or career-related
  7. Meet with Faculty
  8. Receive guidance and support to assist in reaching out to potential employers/alumni
  9. Conduct mock interviews, live and via video
  10. Use space with a reservation for online/phone interviews

Where is the HUB?

The HUB is located in the Life Science Building (LSB) on campus in room 301.
